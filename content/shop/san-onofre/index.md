San Onofre is that slow lane of the endless summer. Old logs, mellow peaks, station wagons in the sand and nobody in a hurry.  
This little zine is a quiet love letter to a day spent there, part of my California beach days.

**Hand-made, signed limited edition of 15 copies.**  
Each one printed and assembled at home, so no two are exactly the same.

- A5 size, 44 pages inside
- 120gsm inside pages, 220gsm cover
- Home printed
- Stitch bound
- Signed and numbered
